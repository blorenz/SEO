Hands On Lab

Sample Dealer

 

Account Executive / District Manager

 

 

 

 

Read and complete this online hands-on-lab. You will need a computer with internet access and a logon account for a demo dealer.

 

Login

  1. Login as a dealer to the demo website.

Search

  1. Search for an item (any industry) using the Advanced Search.
  2. Find an item with a Buy Now option. Read the auction and understand its fees.

Buy

  1. Select the Buy Now option to purchase the item, then see the Bids for Auctions Won screen and verify the item is listed with the correct price.

Verify

  1. View the online invoice for the current month and verify the proper fee is listed (last entry) for the item you just purchased.
  2. Log into your email account and verify a receipt email was sent with the proper information about the item you purchased and who the selling dealer is.

List / Create Auction

  1. Capture some pictures (at least 2 and no more than 5) of an item you would like to create an auction for. A quick location to gather info and pictures of an item is eBay. Copy the pictures to your computer.
  2. Add and item to your inventory and create an auction with acceptable WHOLESALE prices and to start as soon as possible. Allow your auction to run for 14 days.
  3. Once the auction is active (the auction is no longer in Pending status), edit the auction and lower the reserve price by $500.00.

Verify

  1. View the auction in My Wholesale Auctions.
  2. Check your email again to verify the item was created with the appropriate details about the auction.
