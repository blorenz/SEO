CarFAX Joins Technology with Go DEALER to DEALER for Online Wholesale Auctions

Go DEALER to DEALER today announced their dealer customers will have access to Carfax Vehicle History Reports for every car it sells.  This is the first step in dealers protecting themselves against buying used vehicles with costly hidden problems.
David Smalley, Chief Information Officer of Go DEALER to DEALER says "This project adds value to each auction by enabling sellers the ability to extend their CarFAX contract into their online wholesale market place at no additional cost. Also bidders can see the FULL CarFAX report at no cost, as long as the selling dealer has already ran the report. If not, the bidding dealer still has the opportunity to purchase the full report. This service is available to every auto and truck auction, nationwide."
Lewis Bungard, founder and CEO of Go DEALER to DEALER says "we are excited about our new relationship with CarFAX and the unique program we have created. Our business is expanding in the automotive, marine, power sports and RV industries due to great business relationships like the one we formed with CarFAX. "
Bungard founded the company in 2000 to respond to the challenges of locating, buying and selling wholesale inventory as well as the high cost and time of transporting units to physical auctions. Go DEALER to DEALER, based in Gahanna, Ohio, has created a wholesale venue for dealers to list, buy and sell wholesale inventory 24/7. Now, using a fully automated web-based application, dealers can buy and sell wholesale inventory from any computer with Internet access.
