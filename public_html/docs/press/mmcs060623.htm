MMCS, Inc Signs Distribution Agreement with Go DEALER to DEALER

Motorcycle Management Consulting Services, Inc., a California based consulting firm, has joined forces with Go DEALER to DEALER to introduce the PowerSports industry to a state-of-the-art online wholesale auction service.

Responding to the challenges of locating, buying and selling inventory and the high cost of traveling to physical auctions, Go DEALER to DEALER has created a venue for dealers to list, buy and sell inventory 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Now, using a fully automated web-based application, dealers can buy and sell from any computer with internet access.

MMCS, Inc., working with its national network of agents, is proud to offer this innovative solution designed to meet another challenge faced by PowerSports dealers. Julia McCarthy, President of MMCS, Inc. states that "it's interesting that we frequently hear dealers comment about a lack of inventory while others comment about a surplus. Helping those dealers find each other has been a challenge until now. With Go DEALER to DEALER we bring the dealers with a surplus directly to the dealers in need without the cost of travel or transporting inventory."

McCarthy says "There are NO membership fees, NO sell fees and NO listing fees for dealers, and dealers feeding PowerSports inventory to the current national PowerSports auction venues should pay particular attention to the "No Sell Fee"policy."Go DEALER to DEALER also offers a wholesale venue for financial, fleet, lease and rental companies giving corporate remarketers the ability to save millions of dollars in reduced sell fee costs.

The only fees for dealers participating in the on-line auction are the buy fees paid by the dealer buying a vehicle. These fees are comparable to those charged at a physical auction but the dealer doesn't need to leave his dealership to buy and/or sell. Also, with Go DEALER to DEALER, the dealer can have his vehicles on the showroom floor available for retail sale and as part of a wholesale auction - at the same time!!!

Lewis F. Bungard, CEO of DEALER to DEALER, L.L.C. ( www.godealertodealer.com ) comments that "the site was designed by dealers for dealers, and as the owner of a dealership, I was tired of the unnecessary transportation cost due to inventory not selling at the physical auctions." Bungard continues “that's why, from the inception of the idea of godealertodealer.com, we knew the value and huge savings to bringing this incredible tool to dealers on a national level."

"We are excited about the relationship with MMCS, Inc and how MMCS will be helping the PowerSports industry by filling the voids that the industry has suffered for decades. Our business is exploding in all industries due to great business relationships like MMCS, Inc. and others"

MMCS, Inc., and Go DEALER to DEALER bring the wholesale auctions to you!
